About

Welcome to Sauro Active Paws! I’m Sauro, an experienced dog walker dedicated to making your dog’s day brighter and more fulfilling.

As a lifelong dog enthusiast and owner of a sweet rescue pup named Casper, I bring a deep understanding of dogs' unique personalities and needs.

Whether it’s a playful puppy or a seasoned companion, I tailor every walk to suit your dog’s temperament, ensuring a safe and joyful experience.

My Mission To provide cost-effective and loving care for your furry friend.
